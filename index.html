The Name

ملال / Malal

Malal is an old word for boredom — the slow, grey feeling of doing the same thing on repeat. We named the brand after the thing we're against.

Every piece is designed to break a pattern, not fit one. No two drops repeat. No two prints match. If it feels routine, we scrap it.
